## About me
 
I'm an undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering at **Mymensingh Engineering College**, affiliated with the University of Dhaka. My primary focus is on **embedded systems**, **PCB design**, and **robotics** — building circuits that interact with the physical world.
 
I enjoy working hands-on: most of my projects are built on Veroboard prototypes, where I design, solder, and debug hardware end-to-end. I also have a growing interest in **VLSI design**, and recently had the opportunity to visit **Ulkasemi** on an official college industrial tour — a meaningful exposure to the semiconductor industry.

## Featured projects
 
### Soccer Bot
An autonomous soccer-playing robot built for competitive university robotics events. Designed the motor driver circuit, integrated sensors for ball detection, and implemented motion logic in firmware. Hardware constructed on Veroboard.
 
### Cozmo Clench Bot
A gripping/clamping robot mechanism built for object manipulation tasks. Involved mechanical linkage design, servo control, and circuit prototyping on Veroboard.
